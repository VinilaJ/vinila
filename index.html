About Me

I am an Associate Professor (Grade‑2) with the Department of Software Systems, School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE), Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore. I have over 19 years of academic experience. My teaching and research focus on Data Mining, Network Security, and Image Processing. I have published 45+ international journal papers and 40+ conference papers, and have served as resource person, organizer, and committee member for numerous academic programs.
